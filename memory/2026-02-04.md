## Calculation Formula
**Dry Equivalent LBS** = (Trimmed Weight grams / 453) + (Frozen weight grams / 453) * 15%

Where:
- Trimmed Weight = Sum of `Weight` field from `Trimmed_Weights` tab for the week
- Frozen Weight = Sum of `Frozen Weight` field from `2025_Harvest_Sheet` for the week
- Week = Monday to Sunday range

## Week Selection Logic
**Report uses the last 6 Mondays** (not including current week)

For report run on Tuesday Feb 3, 2026:
- Last Monday was Feb 2, 2026
- Use weeks starting: 12/22, 12/29, 1/5, 1/12, 1/19, 1/26
