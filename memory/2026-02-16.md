## Bonsai App YTD Discrepancy — ROOT CAUSE FOUND

Aaron reported: prod chart email shows YTD 1,723.25 lbs, but Bonsai app shows 3,079.59 lbs.

**Root cause:** The `2026_Trimmer_Tracker` and `2026_Harvest_Sheet` tabs contain **3,466 rows from late 2025** (starting Nov 24, 2025). These are harvests that started in 2025 but were trimmed across the year boundary.

- **weekly_report.py** (prod chart email): Filters by date — only counts Jan 1 2026+. **Correct: 1,723.25 lbs**
- **app/lib/sheets.ts** (Bonsai app): Reads ALL rows from `2026_*` tabs with no year filter. **Incorrect: 3,079.59 lbs** (includes ~1,356 lbs of 2025 data)

**Breakdown:**
- Buds from 2025 rows in 2026 tab: 1,313.28 lbs (594,916g)
- Buds from 2026 rows: 1,639.62 lbs (742,746g)
- Frozen from 2025: 287.01 lbs
- Frozen from 2026: 557.55 lbs

**Fix needed:** Add year filter to `processYear()` in `sheets.ts` — filter on parsed date >= Jan 1 of target year. The `yearFilter` param exists in `getWk()` but is never passed when calling `processYear()` for 2026.

**Status:** Diagnosed, awaiting Aaron's go-ahead to fix.
