# 2026-02-09 - Weekly Production Report Built

## What We Did
Built the Bonsai Weekly Production Report email from scratch, replacing the Nestflow AI version.

## Data Sources (FINAL - 2026 tabs only)
- **2026_Trimmer_Tracker** → Buds (Weight column), Plants Trimmed, Sick Plants, Rooms Trimmed, Conversion Rate (with GWBP lookup)
- **2026_Harvest_Sheet** → Frozen LBS, Plants Harvested, Frozen Plants, GWBP LBS
- **2026_Trim** → Smalls, Trim, Mold/B-Tier

## Table Rows (in order)
1. Buds (lbs)
2. Smalls (lbs)
3. Trim (lbs)
4. Mold / B-Tier (lbs)
5. Frozen LBS
6. Plants Harvested (count)
7. Frozen Plants (count)
8. Plants Trimmed (count, excludes sick)
9. Sick Plants (count, column F = 'Y')
10. Grams Per Plant (buds g / plants trimmed)
11. Conversion Rate (buds g / GWBP g of trimmed plants)
12. GWBP LBS
13. Room Trimmed (unique rooms that week, joined with &)
14. Dry Equivalent LBS (buds + frozen * 15%)
15. Goal LBS (250 per week, subject to change)
16. Delta (dry equiv - goal)

## Table Layout
- Dates across columns (last 6 Mondays, Mon-Sun weeks)
- 6-Wk Total column (before avg)
- 6-Wk Avg column (last)
- Alternating light green/white row stripes
- 6-Wk Total calculations:
  - Most rows: simple sum
  - Grams/Plant: total buds g / total plants trimmed (from 6-wk sums)
  - Conversion Rate: total buds g / total GWBP g (from 6-wk sums)
  - Room Trimmed: blank

## Email Format
- Summary paragraph (dry equiv, delta, conversion, wow change)
- Room performance cards (horizontal, equal width)
- Production table with alternating rows
- Chart 1: 6-week bar chart (matplotlib, CID embedded)
- Chart 2: 2025 vs 2026 year-over-year comparison (matplotlib, CID embedded)
- YTD summary footer
- Sent from bonsaiburner420bot@gmail.com
