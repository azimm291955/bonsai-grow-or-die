### What It Does
Auto-generates smart production insights from weekly data:
- Week-over-week production change (% + direction arrow)
- Goal streak tracking (consecutive weeks above 250 lb target)
- Sick rate alerts (thresholds at 5% warning, 10% critical)
- Rolling 4-week momentum (recent avg vs prior 4-week avg)
- Flower ratio quality check (premium mix indicator at 90%+, low at <70%)
- Plant throughput changes (±50 plants flagged)

Each insight card is color-coded by sentiment (green/amber/red borders) in a responsive grid.
