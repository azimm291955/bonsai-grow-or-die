# Bonsai: Grow or Die
## Perfect Play Analysis & Maximum Cash Projection

**Game Designer Assessment & Financial Optimization Report**

---

## Executive Summary

With **perfect play**, a player can achieve approximately **$39 million in cash** by the win date (April 20, 2026).

This represents an optimal strategy combining:
- Strategic facility expansion (all 9 rooms by 2021)
- Prioritized upgrade investments (Operations, Genetics, Lighting first)
- Market-aware decision making (aggressive growth in 2021, conservation in 2018)
- Pre-roll monetization (critical revenue driver from 2022 onward)

**Range of Realistic Outcomes:**
- Conservative Strategy: $5M - $8M
- Realistic Optimized: **$39M** (Perfect Play)
- Theoretical Maximum: $45M+ (extremely difficult)

## Game Length & Economics

**Duration:** December 30, 2015 → April 20, 2026 (≈10.33 years)
- **Starting Capital:** $765,000
- **Base Cycle Time:** 96 game days (32 veg + 64 flower)
- **Harvests Per Room (over game):** 41-44 harvests depending on upgrades

### Market Context
The game simulates a real cannabis market with distinct phases:

1. **2015-2016:** Establishment phase, high prices ($2,007 → $1,880/lb)
2. **2017-2018:** Market crash, survival test ($1,471 → $759/lb)
3. **2019-2021:** Recovery and peak opportunity ($999 → $1,721/lb)
4. **2022-2026:** Commodity compression & margin war ($948 → $648/lb)

This creates strategic decision points where timing is everything.

## Upgrade Prioritization for Perfect Play

### Tier 1: MUST HAVE (Year 1-2)
**Operations T1+** ($100K+)
- Auto-flip saves constant manual flipping
- Rotation speed bonus helps with late harvests
- Essential quality-of-life feature

**Genetics T1-T2** ($100K-$250K)
- +10% → +22% yield bonus
- +5% → +10% price bonus
- **ROI: ~$50K-$100K per harvest = 200%+ return**

### Tier 2: HIGH VALUE (Year 2-3)
**Genetics T3** ($500K)
- Final +35% yield (critical for 2018 crash survival)
- +12% price bonus
- **Essential before Q3 2018**

**Lighting T1-T2** ($120K-$280K)
- +8% → +18% yield
- -15% → -28% electricity costs
- **Breakeven in 3-4 harvests, pure profit after**

### Tier 3: MEDIUM VALUE (Year 3-4)
**Operations T2-T3** ($200K-$350K)
- Additional cycle time savings (5+ days)
- More harvests over game lifetime = +2.3 per room
- **9 rooms × 2.3 harvests × $466K = $9.7M value**

**Pre-roll T1-T3** ($75K-$380K)
- Trim monetization: $145 → $400 → $800 per lb
- Year 1-2: Minimal value (few rooms)
- **Year 3+: $3M+ annual revenue at T3**

### Tier 4: LOWER VALUE (Invest after Phase 3)
**Environmental & Irrigation** ($770K + $700K)
- Reduce overhead costs (-20% to -66%)
- Better ROI in late game when every dollar matters
- Early game: overhead is fixed, can't optimize much

## How to Play Imperfectly (and Why It Happens)

- Unlock rooms 5-9 before cash allows

- Result: $3M-$8M final cash

### Mistake 2: Wrong Upgrade Priorities
- Maxing Irrigation/Environmental before Genetics
- Saves 20% labor/nutrients but costs 20x more
- Genetics saves 35% of output (worth $200K+/harvest)

### Mistake 3: Missing Pre-Roll Window
- Forget to build Pre-roll until 2024 (too late)
- Market already crashed, base commodity price is $649/lb

- Enjoy immediate $500K+ infusion
- Pay 40% revenue penalty forever

- Miss a harvest, crops sitting 5+ days after ready

- 9 rooms: -$4M+ total if systematic
- Result: $15M-$25M final cash

## Game Design Insights

### What Makes the Game Interesting?
1. **Market Cycles:** Players must adapt strategy (growth → survival → growth)
2. **Opportunity Cost:** Every dollar spent = opportunity deferred
3. **Upgrade Scaling:** Later upgrades cost 40% more (discourages maxing early)
4. **Time Crunch:** Game ends at fixed date, can't wait forever
5. **Parallel Gameplay:** Multiple rooms enable strategic pacing

### What Could Be Balanced Better?
1. **Pre-Roll Timing:** Strongly penalizes players who unlock late
   - Maybe give pre-roll T1 scaling bonus in 2023-2025?
2. **Environmental/Irrigation:** Weak ROI early, strong late
   - Could reduce early tier costs or increase yield mods
3. **Market Predictability:** Players who know 2021 peak win big
   - Could randomize AMR slightly each year
4. **Operations T3 Power:** Saves 5 days (5%) but costs $350K
   - Is that cost justified? Probably yes, but tight

### Game Difficulty Progression
- **Early (2016):** Managing multiple rooms (high engagement)
- **Mid (2017-2018):** Surviving crash (high stress)
- **Peak (2021):** Capital allocation (strategic puzzle)
- **Late (2022-2026):** Optimizing margins (lower engagement)

The difficulty curve could be smoother - late game is less engaging.
