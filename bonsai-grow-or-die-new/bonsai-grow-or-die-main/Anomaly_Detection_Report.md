## Methodology

1. **Grouping:** Data grouped by 'Combine' (batch/lot identifier)
2. **Statistical Measures:** Calculated Mean and Standard Deviation for each Combine group
3. **Z-Score Calculation:** `Z = (Individual Weight - Group Mean) / Group Std Dev`
4. **Anomaly Threshold:** Z-Score > 2 (indicates weight is more than 2 standard deviations above the group average, representing >95.4% confidence level)
