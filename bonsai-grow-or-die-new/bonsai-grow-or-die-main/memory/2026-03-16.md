### Details
Built a "Weekly Snapshot" component that generates copy-ready performance reports in two formats:
- **Executive**: concise summary (production, goal status, WoW change, quality metrics, YTD pace)
- **Detailed**: comprehensive breakdown (rooms, strains, trim team, annual projections, goal hit rate)

Features:
- Collapsible card in the Dashboard (between Weekly Momentum and Week in Review)
- One-click "Copy to Clipboard" with visual feedback
- Format toggle between executive and detailed views
- Plain-text output that works in Slack, email, etc.

### Why It Matters
Fills a real gap for a CFO — sharing weekly updates with stakeholders who don't log into the app. Previously required manually compiling numbers from the dashboard. Now it's one click.
