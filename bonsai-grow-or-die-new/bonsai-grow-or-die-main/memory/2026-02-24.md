## 🐛 Harvest Page Client-Side Crash — Fixed

**Bug:** Harvest (and Trim) tabs were crashing with "Application error: a client-side exception" on mobile.

**Root cause:** React Rules of Hooks violation — two `useMemo` calls were placed *after* an early `return` (the loading spinner). React saw 3 hooks on render #1 (loading) and 5 hooks on render #2 (data ready), causing an immediate client-side crash.

**Fix:** Moved both `useMemo` calls before the early return, with `if (!data) return []` guards inside them.
