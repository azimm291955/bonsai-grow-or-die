### What It Does
Full room utilization analysis answering "are we using our facility efficiently?":

**Facility-wide summary cards:**
- Avg utilization % (active grow time vs total cycle)
- Avg cycle time (harvest-to-harvest days)
- Avg turns per year per room
- Annualized total output at current pace

**Utilization by Room (stacked horizontal bar):**
- Green = active grow time, gray = idle/transition
- Shows which rooms have the most downtime

**Annualized g/sq ft (horizontal bar):**
- Not just per-turn yield — accounts for turn frequency too
- Faster-turning rooms rank higher even with slightly lower per-turn yield

**Cycle Time Trend (line chart):**
- Per-room cycle times across turns
- 56-day reference line (theoretical minimum = flower phase only)
- Shows whether rooms are getting faster or slower

**Detailed Efficiency Table:**
- Per-room: turns, avg cycle, idle days, utilization %, turns/yr, avg lbs, annual g/ft²
- Color-coded utilization bars and idle day thresholds

### Why It Matters
- CFO-level insight: facility efficiency directly impacts production capacity
- Annualized g/sq ft is a powerful metric — combines yield quality + operational speed
- Identifies rooms with excessive downtime (opportunity to squeeze more production)
- Cycle time trends reveal whether operational improvements are working
